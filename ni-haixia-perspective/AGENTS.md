## Workspace Rules

This directory is a self-contained Codex skill generated by `huashu-nuwa`.

### Structure

- `SKILL.md`: the final runnable skill.
- `references/research/`: dimension-specific research notes. All research findings must be written here before synthesis.
- `references/sources/`: source materials gathered during research.
  - `books/`: book excerpts, bibliographies, or legally available public text notes.
  - `transcripts/`: lecture, interview, and video transcript notes.
  - `articles/`: article and webpage notes.
- `scripts/`: helper scripts copied or adapted from the Nuwa generator.

### Naming

- Research files are numbered `01-` through `06-` to match the Nuwa workflow.
- Source notes should use lowercase pinyin or English slugs plus source type, for example `tianji-lecture-notes.md`.

### Cleanup

- Temporary downloads, raw command output, and failed scrape artifacts do not belong in this directory.
- If a source cannot be verified, record it in the relevant research file as low-confidence instead of saving untrusted raw content.
