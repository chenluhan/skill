  - clinics claiming transmission,
  - memorial/fan sites,
  - renewed social-media disputes over credentials and medical claims.

## Time-Based Interpretation

- Early phase: apprenticeship and metaphysical / `Tian Ji` teaching.
- Middle phase: U.S. clinical institution + board/acupuncture legitimacy.
- Late phase: `Ren Ji` corpus completion + public polemic + mainland/overseas dissemination.
- Posthumous phase: fragmented, often unauthorized circulation; source quality varies widely.
